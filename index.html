About Me

Neelesh is a Data Science and Analytics enthusiast with a good mix of business understanding and application of advanced analytical techniques in tools/languages like Python, R, and SQL. At Tiger Analytics, he worked with an eclectic mix of clients ranging from Automobiles to Consumer-Packaged-Goods. He was responsible for deploying predictive models using machine learning algorithms to gauge global performance and deliver business insights. Earlier, he worked on power spectral analysis using Fast Fourier Transforms on data generated from multiple edge devices during his stint at GyanData. He also worked with a couple of startups and provided data-driven consulting. He graduated with a Bachelor of Engineering in Industrial Engineering specializing in Data Science from the College of Engineering Guindy.
